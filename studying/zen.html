Being Zen

Studying can often be stressful, and stress and anxiety can negatively impact our ability to learn and retain information. When we are zen, we are able to manage stress and anxiety, allowing us to focus better on our studies.

Breathing Exercises

Breathing exercises can be an effective way to promote a zen state by helping to calm the mind and reduce stress and anxiety.

Nature Sounds

Nature sounds, such as the sound of waves, birds chirping, or rain falling, can be soothing to the mind and help promote relaxation and a sense of calm

Yoga

Yoga is an great way to promote a zen state by combining physical movements and meditation techniques that can help to reduce stress and anxiety.
