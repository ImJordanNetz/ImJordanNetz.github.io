R U B I K S cube

Jordan Netz

A Rubik's Cube is a three-dimensional mechanical puzzle invented by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Erno Rubik in 1974. The Rubik's Cube has become one of the best-selling toys in history, with over 350 million sold worldwide. It has also spawn communities of "speedcubers" who race to solve the puzzle as quickly as possible.
